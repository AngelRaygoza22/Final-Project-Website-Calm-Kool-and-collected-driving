About Us

Calm Kool AND Collected Driving Lessons does not only include driving courses for normal beginner drivers but also for those with exotic sport cars. We specialize in making you the best driver you can possibly be as well as trying to make the roads alot safer. We want to get more people to learn how to drive and to help those who just recently bought an exotic car or have had an exotic car for some time and do not know how to drive it properly. Our team provides great tips that you can use even after taking our courses.
